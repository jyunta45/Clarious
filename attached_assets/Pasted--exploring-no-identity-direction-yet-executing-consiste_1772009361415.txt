"exploring"     → no identity direction yet
"executing"     → consistent goals, high follow-through  
"restructuring" → direction exists but repeated struggles
"developing"    → default, healthy middle ground
```

This is the kind of signal that makes responses feel calibrated without the AI needing to figure it out each time. The thresholds are conservative — `goalConsistency > 0.7` is a high bar, `recurringStruggles > 2` is real friction. No hallucination risk because it's pure computation from existing data.
```
